Our African Story

Born in Liberia. Built on tradition. Sharing African craftsmanship with the world.

From Monrovia to the World

AfriSteps was founded by a school principal in Liberia who wanted to share the beauty of African artisan craftsmanship with the world — while creating income opportunities for local artisans.

Every pair of slippers in our collection is made by skilled craftspeople across West Africa, using traditional techniques passed down through generations combined with bold, modern African prints.

When you buy from AfriSteps, you're not just getting a beautiful pair of shoes — you're supporting African families and keeping ancient traditions alive.
